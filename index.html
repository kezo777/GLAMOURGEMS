About Us

GLAMOURGEMS is a secret society of jewellers who are specializing in creating exquisite and unique pieces of jewellery for the elite.
We use only the finest materials, such as diamonds, rubies, sapphires, emeralds, and pearls. We also incorporate rare and exotic elements, such as meteorites, fossils, and ancient artifacts. Our craftsmanship is unmatched, and our designs are inspired by art, history, and nature.
GLAMOURGEMS operate in the shadows, and only accept clients who are referred by their existing members. We not advertise our services, nor we have a physical store. We communicate with our clients through encrypted messages via NOSTR client via decentralised messaging app IRIS, and deliver our products in discreet way. We also require our clients to pay with Bitcoin, the most popular cryptocurrency in the world.
Bitcoin is the ideal payment method for GLAMOURGEMS, as it offers many benefits, especially privacy. Some of the benefits of Bitcoin are: it is a decentralized, secure, fast, transparent, and anonymous cryptocurrency. No central authority is in control. A network of worldwide distributed computers verifies transactions. Cryptography and blockchain prevent fraud. Low fees and limited supply attract users. Multiple addresses and mixing services enhance privacy. GLAMOURGEMS and their clients trust and value Bitcoin.
